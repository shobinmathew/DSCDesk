Refund & Cancellation Policy

At DSC Desk, we strive to provide fast and reliable Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) services. Since DSC processing involves verification and submission to licensed Certifying Authorities (CA), refunds and cancellations are subject to the terms outlined below.

1. Cancellation Before Document Submission

If you request cancellation before submitting any documents for verification, your order may be eligible for cancellation. A small processing fee may apply.

2. No Cancellation After Document Submission

Once documents are submitted for verification (Aadhaar eKYC, PAN, address proof, etc.), the application is forwarded to the Certifying Authority. At this stage, cancellation is not possible.

3. No Refund After Application Processing

Refunds are not applicable once:

  • Your DSC application is submitted to the Certifying Authority
  • Verification has started or been completed
  • Video KYC has been completed
  • DSC has been issued

4. Refunds for Duplicate or Failed Payments

Refunds are issued in the following cases:

  • Duplicate payment made by the customer
  • Payment deducted but order not created
  • Technical issues resulting in failed transactions

Refunds for such cases are processed within 5–7 working days.

5. Incorrect or Invalid Documents

If your application is rejected due to incorrect or invalid documents, no refund is applicable. You may resubmit correct documents without additional charges (if allowed by CA).

6. Token / USB Device Refunds

If a USB token has been shipped, refunds are not possible. If the token is defective, it will be replaced as per manufacturer warranty.
