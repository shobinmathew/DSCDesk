Document Safety Policy

At DSC Desk, your privacy and data safety are extremely important to us. We are an Authorized Partner / Sub‑RA of eMudhra for Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) services. All Aadhaar eKYC, document verification, and DSC issuance are performed securely on the official eMudhra portal as per CCA guidelines.

1. Information We Collect

DSC Desk does not collect or store any identity documents such as Aadhaar, PAN, address proof, photographs, or signatures. We only collect the basic details required to create your DSC order:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address

All further verification is completed directly on the Certifying Authority (CA) portal.

2. No Document Upload on Our Website

We do not ask you to upload any documents on our website. All Aadhaar eKYC, PAN verification, and document submission happen securely on the CA portal.

Since DSC Desk is an Authorized eMudhra Partner, all verification steps are completed directly on the eMudhra system. We do not collect or store any identity documents.

3. How Verification Happens

Once your order is created, you receive a secure link from the Certifying Authority (CA) to complete:

  • Aadhaar OTP eKYC
  • Video KYC (if required)
  • Document upload (if required by CA)

DSC Desk does not access or store any of these documents.

4. No Storage of Sensitive Documents

Since all verification happens directly on the CA portal:

  • We do not store your Aadhaar
  • We do not store your PAN
  • We do not store your address proof
  • We do not store your photo or signature

5. Compliance With Legal Standards

All verification and document handling are performed by licensed Certifying Authorities in compliance with:

  • CCA (Controller of Certifying Authorities) guidelines
  • IT Act 2000
  • Certifying Authority (CA) verification rules

6. Your Rights

Since DSC Desk does not store your documents, you may directly request the Certifying Authority for:

  • Deletion of uploaded documents
  • Correction of personal details
  • Information on how your documents were used
