Privacy Policy

At DSC Desk, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, transparent, and secure experience when applying for Digital Signature Certificates (DSC). DSC Desk operates as an Authorized Partner / Sub‑RA of eMudhra, one of India’s leading Certifying Authorities (CA). All identity verification, Aadhaar eKYC, and document uploads happen directly on the official eMudhra portal.

1. Information We Collect

DSC Desk follows a strict “minimum data collection” policy. We only collect the basic details required to create your DSC order:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address

We do not collect or store Aadhaar, PAN, address proof, photographs, signatures, or any identity documents.

2. How Verification Happens

Once your order is created, you receive a secure link from eMudhra to complete:

  • Aadhaar OTP eKYC
  • Video KYC (if required)
  • Document upload (if required by eMudhra)

All verification steps occur on the eMudhra portal. DSC Desk does not access or store your documents at any stage.

3. How We Use Your Information

The basic information you provide (name, mobile, email) is used only for:

  • Creating your DSC order
  • Sending you the eMudhra verification link
  • Communicating updates regarding your DSC application

4. No Document Storage

Since all verification happens on the eMudhra portal:

  • We do not store Aadhaar
  • We do not store PAN
  • We do not store address proof
  • We do not store photos or signatures

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share your information with any third party except:

  • eMudhra (Certifying Authority) for DSC issuance
  • Regulatory bodies when legally required

6. Compliance With Legal Standards

All verification and DSC issuance follow:

  • CCA (Controller of Certifying Authorities) guidelines
  • IT Act 2000
  • eMudhra CA verification rules

7. Your Rights

Since DSC Desk does not store your documents, you may directly request eMudhra for:

  • Deletion of uploaded documents
  • Correction of personal details
  • Information on how your documents were used
