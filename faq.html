Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are the most common questions customers ask about Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) and our services at DSC Desk. We are an Authorized Partner / Sub‑RA of eMudhra, ensuring secure and government‑approved DSC issuance.

1. What is a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)?

A DSC is a secure digital key issued by a Certifying Authority (CA) such as eMudhra. It is used for signing documents, filing GST, MCA, Income Tax, PF, and other online services.

2. Are you authorized to issue DSC?

Yes. DSC Desk is an Authorized Partner / Sub‑RA of eMudhra. All verification and DSC issuance happen through the official eMudhra portal.

3. How can I verify that you are an eMudhra Partner?

Customers can verify our partnership in the following ways:

  • You will receive your DSC verification link from https://services.e-mudhra.com
  • You may email eMudhra at support@emudhra.com to confirm our Partner ID
  • Only authorized partners can create DSC orders on the eMudhra RA portal

4. What information do you collect?

We follow a strict “minimum data collection” policy. We only collect:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address

We do not collect Aadhaar, PAN, address proof, or any documents. All verification happens directly on the eMudhra portal.

5. How does the DSC application process work?

The process is simple:

  • You provide your name, mobile, and email
  • You receive a secure eMudhra link
  • You complete Aadhaar OTP eKYC or Video KYC
  • eMudhra issues your DSC

6. How long does it take to get a DSC?

Most DSCs are issued within 10–30 minutes after successful eKYC. Video KYC may take slightly longer depending on CA processing.

7. Do you store my documents?

No. DSC Desk does not store Aadhaar, PAN, address proof, or any documents. All verification happens securely on the eMudhra portal.

8. What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available only for:

  • Duplicate payments
  • Failed transactions

Once documents are submitted on the eMudhra portal or verification has started, refunds are not applicable.
